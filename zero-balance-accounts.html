Top Zero Balance Digital Savings Accounts

1. Kotak 811 Digital Savings Account

  • Zero balance account
  • Instant online account opening via Aadhaar + PAN
  • Earn up to 3.5% interest p.a.
  • Virtual debit card provided instantly
  • Upgrade to full KYC for higher limits

2. SBI Digital Savings Account (YONO)

  • Zero balance with limited KYC (upgrade later)
  • Open via SBI YONO app
  • Access to full SBI branch network
  • Free virtual debit card
  • Interest rate around 2.7% p.a.

3. AU Small Finance Bank Digital Account

  • Zero balance account via AU 0101 app
  • Up to 7.25% interest on higher balances
  • Video KYC enabled
  • Free virtual + physical debit card

4. Axis ASAP Digital Account

  • Zero balance account via mobile app
  • Instant virtual debit card
  • Interest rate ~3% to 3.5% p.a.
  • UPI, mobile banking, and full digital access

5. IDFC FIRST Bank Zero Balance Account

  • Zero balance with full KYC
  • Up to 7% interest (on select balances)
  • Free Visa Platinum debit card
  • No hidden charges, great customer support

6. Paytm Payments Bank

  • Instant digital account via Paytm app
  • Zero balance & zero charges
  • UPI & wallet integration
  • Interest rate ~2.5% p.a.
  • RBI-regulated (but no physical branches)

How to Choose the Right Account

  • Interest rate: Higher returns for idle money
  • App experience: Look for simple, stable UI
  • Free features: Debit card, transfers, ATM access
  • Security: Check for RBI registration and KYC policies
